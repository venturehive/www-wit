WIT Virtual Training Program


15 female entrepreneurs and executives have the opportunity to participate in a 4-month educational program to scale their businesses and develop new skills to run world-class companies while also giving them the opportunity to showcase their businesses at WPC 2016 in Toronto.

If selected, you will participate in a 4-week intensive entrepreneurship development program and additional monthly mentoring sessions all run by Venture Hive, a leading entrepreneurship education company based in Miami, and a Microsoft Partner.
